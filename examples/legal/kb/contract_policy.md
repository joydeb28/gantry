# Legal Review Policy

## Contract Review Standards

All contracts above £10,000 in value must be reviewed by the legal team before signing.

Standard review checklist:
- Liability cap: must not exceed 2x contract value
- Indemnification clauses: mutual indemnification required
- Intellectual property: company retains all IP developed under contract
- Data processing agreement: required for any contract involving personal data
- Governing law: UK law preferred; US law acceptable for US counterparties
- Termination for convenience: 30-day notice minimum
- Auto-renewal clauses: must be flagged and approved by procurement

## Compliance Checks

### GDPR
- Any contract involving EU personal data must include a Data Processing Agreement
- Data transfers outside EEA require appropriate safeguards (SCCs or adequacy decision)
- Breach notification obligations must be specified (72-hour window)

### SOX
- Financial reporting vendors must provide SOC 2 Type II report
- Access controls and audit logging must be contractually guaranteed

## Dispute Resolution

All disputes must first go through a 30-day mediation period before litigation.
Arbitration is preferred over litigation for contracts under £500,000.
All disputes escalate immediately to the General Counsel's office.
