# Finance & Expense Policy

## Invoice Approval

All invoices must be validated before payment is released.

Validation steps:
1. Confirm invoice matches the purchase order (PO number, amount, line items)
2. Verify vendor is on the approved vendor list
3. Check budget availability in the relevant cost centre
4. Confirm delivery/services have been received (goods receipt)

Approval thresholds:
- Up to £1,000: department manager approval
- £1,001–£10,000: finance controller approval
- £10,001–£50,000: CFO approval
- Above £50,000: board approval required

Invoices over £250 (value_usd > 250) cannot be auto-approved; they require manual sign-off.

## Expense Claims

Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the spend date.

Reimbursable categories:
- Business travel (rail, air, taxis for client meetings)
- Accommodation (hotel up to £200/night)
- Client entertainment (up to £75 per person, pre-approved)
- Home office equipment (up to £500 per year)

All claims over £50 require a VAT receipt.
Receipts must match the claimed amount exactly.

## Budget Management

Quarterly budget reviews are conducted by the finance team.
Departments must flag spend within 10% of budget limit.
Over-budget requests require executive approval.
