# HR Onboarding & Leave Policy

## New Hire Onboarding

All new hires must complete the onboarding process within the first week of joining.
The onboarding kit includes:
- Welcome email with system access credentials
- IT equipment provisioning checklist
- HR handbook and code of conduct
- Benefits enrollment guide
- Manager introduction meeting scheduled within 48 hours

System accounts provisioned on day one:
- Corporate email and calendar
- Slack workspace
- JIRA / project management tool
- GitHub organisation access (engineering roles)
- Expense management portal

## Leave Policy

All leave requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance.

Entitlements:
- Annual leave: 25 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year (self-certified up to 3 consecutive days)
- Compassionate leave: 5 days per bereavement event
- Parental leave: 26 weeks primary / 4 weeks secondary carer

Approval chain:
1. Direct manager approves leave requests up to 5 days
2. HR business partner approval required for requests over 5 days
3. Director approval required for requests over 15 days

Backdated leave requests always require HR business partner approval.
