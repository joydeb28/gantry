# IT Access & Helpdesk Policy

## Access Management

All access requests must be approved by the employee's direct manager.

Provisioning process:
1. Requestor submits access request with business justification
2. Manager approves via the IT portal
3. IT provisions access within 1 business day for standard systems
4. Privileged access (admin, root, production) requires security team review

Access levels:
- Read-only: self-service provisioning via IT portal
- Read-write: manager approval required
- Admin/privileged: manager + security team approval, MFA mandatory

All access is reviewed quarterly; unused accounts are suspended after 90 days.

## Incident Management

Incident priority levels:
- P1 (Critical): complete service outage affecting all users — 15-min response
- P2 (High): major service degradation or >25% users affected — 1-hour response
- P3 (Medium): partial service impact, workaround available — 4-hour response
- P4 (Low): minor issue, cosmetic, single user — next business day

All P1/P2 incidents require an on-call engineer page and incident commander.
Post-incident reviews are mandatory for all P1 incidents.

## Hardware Procurement

Standard hardware is procured through the IT portal.
Lead time: 5–10 business days for standard items.
For urgent requests, contact the IT hardware team directly.

Approved standard laptop models are listed in the IT hardware catalogue.
Specialised hardware (GPU workstations, servers) requires finance sign-off.
